# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect
This application collects:
- TikTok account information (required for authentication)
- Video metadata (title, description, tags)
- Authentication tokens (stored locally)

## 2. How We Use Information
- To authenticate with TikTok API
- To upload videos on your behalf
- No data is stored on external servers

## 3. Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your machine.
Tokens are stored in encrypted format.

## 4. Third-Party Services
This application uses TikTok's official API.
See TikTok's privacy policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/us/privacy-policy

## 5. Contact
For privacy concerns, contact the application developer.
